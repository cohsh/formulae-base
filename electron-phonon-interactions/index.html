Coupling Constants

$$g_{n_1n_2\nu}(\mathbf{k}, \mathbf{q}):= \Braket{n_1, \mathbf{k} + \mathbf{q}|\Delta_{\mathbf{q}\nu}\hat{V}|n_2, \mathbf{k}}$$
$$\Delta_{\mathbf{q}\nu}:=\sum_{\kappa \alpha}e_{\kappa \alpha, \nu}(\mathbf{q}) \frac{\partial}{\partial \tau_{\kappa \alpha}(\mathbf{q})}$$
$$\frac{\partial}{\partial \tau_{\kappa \alpha}(\mathbf{q})} := \sum_{p} e^{i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{R}_p} \frac{\partial}{\partial \tau_{\kappa \alpha, p}}$$
