Bloch Electrons

$$\hat{H}_\mathrm{e} := \hat{T}_\mathrm{e} + \hat{V}_e$$
$$\hat{H}_\mathrm{e}\ket{\psi_{n\mathbf{k}}}=\epsilon_{n\mathbf{k}}\ket{\psi_{n\mathbf{k}}}~(n \in \mathbb{N},~ \mathbf{k} \in \mathbb{DBZ})$$
$$\psi_{n\mathbf{k}}(\mathbf{r} + \mathbf{R})=e^{i\mathbf{k} \cdot \mathbf{R}} \psi_{n\mathbf{k}}(\mathbf{r})$$
$$\psi_{n\mathbf{k}}(\mathbf{r})=\frac{1}{\sqrt{N}}e^{i\mathbf{k} \cdot \mathbf{r}} u_{n\mathbf{k}}(\mathbf{r})$$

$$\Delta_{\mathbf{q}\nu}:=l_{\mathbf{q}\nu}\sum_{\kappa \alpha}\sqrt{\frac{M_0}{M_\kappa}}e_{\kappa \alpha, \nu}(\mathbf{q}) \frac{\partial}{\partial \tau_{\kappa \alpha}(\mathbf{q})}$$
$$g_{n_1n_2\nu}(\mathbf{k}, \mathbf{q}):= \Braket{u_{n_1 \mathbf{k} + \mathbf{q}}|\Delta_{\mathbf{q}\nu}\hat{V}|u_{n_2\mathbf{k}}}$$
$$\tau_{\kappa \alpha, p} := R_{\alpha, p} + \tau_{\kappa\alpha}$$

$$\Delta \hat{\tau}_{\kappa \alpha, p} := \sqrt{\frac{M_0}{M_\kappa}}\frac{1}{\sqrt{N}}\sum_{\mathbf{q} \in \mathbb{BZ}}\sum_{\nu = 1}^{dN_\mathrm{a}} e^{i\mathbf{q} \cdot \mathbf{R}_p}e_{\kappa \alpha, \nu}(\mathbf{q}) \hat{z}_{\mathbf{q}\nu}$$
