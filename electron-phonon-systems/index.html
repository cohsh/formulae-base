Electron Phonon Systems

Direct Lattice

$$\mathbf{A}_d := [\mathbf{a}_1 \cdots \mathbf{a}_d],~(\mathbf{a}_i \in \mathbb{R}^d,~ i = 1, \cdots, d)$$
$$\mathcal{A}[\mathbf{A}_d]:=\{ \mathbf{A}_d \cdot [n_1 \cdots n_d] \mid n_i =-N_i,\cdots, N_i,~ i=1,\cdots, d \}$$
$$N:=|\mathcal{A}[\mathbf{A}_d]|$$

Reciprocal Lattice

$$\mathbf{B}_d := [\mathbf{b}_1 \cdots \mathbf{b}_d],~(\mathbf{b}_i \in \mathbb{R}^d,~ i = 1, \cdots, d)$$
$$\mathcal{B}[\mathbf{B}_d]:=\{ \mathbf{B}_d \cdot \mathbf{n} \mid \mathbf{n} \in \mathbb{Z}^d \}$$
$$\mathbb{BZ}:= \{ \mathbf{B}_d \cdot [f_1 \cdots f_d] \mid f_i = \frac{j}{2N_i},~ i=1,\cdots, d, ~ j=-N_i, \cdots, N_i\}$$

Bloch Electrons

$$\hat{H}_\mathrm{e} := \hat{T}_\mathrm{e} + \hat{V}_e$$
$$\hat{H}_\mathrm{e}\ket{\psi_{n\mathbf{k}}}=\epsilon_{n\mathbf{k}}\ket{\psi_{n\mathbf{k}}}~(n \in \mathbb{N},~ \mathbf{k} \in \mathbb{BZ})$$
$$\psi_{n\mathbf{k}}(\mathbf{r} + \mathbf{R})=e^{i\mathbf{k} \cdot \mathbf{R}} \psi_{n\mathbf{k}}(\mathbf{r})$$
$$\psi_{n\mathbf{k}}(\mathbf{r})=\frac{1}{\sqrt{N}}e^{i\mathbf{k} \cdot \mathbf{r}} u_{n\mathbf{k}}(\mathbf{r})$$

Coupling Constants

$$\frac{\partial}{\partial \tau_{\kappa \alpha}(\mathbf{q})} := \sum_{p=1}^N e^{i\mathbf{q}\cdot \mathbf{R}_p} \frac{\partial}{\partial \tau_{\kappa \alpha, p}}$$
$$\Delta_{\mathbf{q}\nu}:=l_{\mathbf{q}\nu}\sum_{\kappa \alpha}\sqrt{\frac{M_0}{M_\kappa}}e_{\kappa \alpha, \nu}(\mathbf{q}) \frac{\partial}{\partial \tau_{\kappa \alpha}(\mathbf{q})}$$
$$g_{n_1n_2\nu}(\mathbf{k}, \mathbf{q}):= \Braket{u_{n_1 \mathbf{k} + \mathbf{q}}|\Delta_{\mathbf{q}\nu}\hat{V}|u_{n_2\mathbf{k}}}$$
$$\tau_{\kappa \alpha, p} := R_{\alpha, p} + \tau_{\kappa\alpha}$$
$$\hat{z}_{\mathbf{q}\nu}:=l_{\mathbf{q}\nu}(\hat{a}_{\mathbf{q}\nu} + \hat{a}^\dagger_{-\mathbf{q}\nu})$$
$$\Delta \hat{\tau}_{\kappa \alpha, p} := \sqrt{\frac{M_0}{M_\kappa}}\frac{1}{\sqrt{N}}\sum_{\mathbf{q} \in \mathbb{BZ}}\sum_{\nu = 1}^{dN_\mathrm{a}} e^{i\mathbf{q} \cdot \mathbf{R}_p}e_{\kappa \alpha, \nu}(\mathbf{q}) \hat{z}_{\mathbf{q}\nu}$$
